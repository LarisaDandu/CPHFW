Copenhagen Fashion Week 2025 is making waves in the fashion world! This year’s focus is sustainability, inspiring the industry to adopt eco-friendly practices. The fashion sector accounts for about 10% of global carbon emissions and 20% of wastewater, highlighting the urgent need for change. As awareness of environmental issues grows, Copenhagen is leading the way toward a greener future in fashion. “Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak." Many of us think that fashion applies just the clothes we wear, but it can be applied to almost anything we do. It’s used to describe a means of expression through colors, textures and styles. This year, the spotlight is on unique fashion pieces from Nordic designers. Copenhagen encourages everyone to change their shopping habits. Thrift shopping is becoming popular, inviting fashion lovers to find affordable, one-of-a-kind items in second-hand stores. This helps reduce waste and supports sustainability. Copenhagen Fashion Week is more than just a showcase; it’s an educational platform. Throughout the week, workshops and discussions focus on sustainable fashion practices. Industry leaders, environmentalists, and designers share their insights, emphasizing the need for sustainability in fashion.
